Functional Tests - Basic, Manual Smoke Test

Prep:

a. copy KLineEd\KLineEdCmdApp\mytest.ksx to KLineEd\KLineEdCmdApp\bin\Debug\netcoreapp2.2

1. Changing Display Cols
------------------------

--edit mytest.ksx --display cols=25 
   compare with KLineEd\FunctionalTestResults\mytestDisplayCols25.jpg
save
   compare KLineEd\KLineEdCmdApp\bin\Debug\netcoreapp2.2\mytest.ksx body section with mytestBodyDisplayCols25.jpg

--edit mytest.ksx --display cols=65
    compare with KLineEd\FunctionalTestResults\mytestDisplayCols65.jpg
save
	compare KLineEd\KLineEdCmdApp\mytest.ksx with KLineEd\KLineEdCmdApp\bin\Debug\netcoreapp2.2\mytest.ksx - should be identical


2. Deleting paraBreak
---------------------

--edit mytest.ksx --display cols=65

move cursor to line 12, col 12
press Del
   compare with KLineEd\FunctionalTestResults\mytestAddR12C12-Del.jpg
press CR
    compare with KLineEd\FunctionalTestResults\mytestDisplayCols65.jpg

3. Adding and deleting characters before end of line
----------------------------------------------------

--edit mytest.ksx --display cols=65

move cursor to line 11, col 12
press INS
type ABCDE
   compare with KLineEd\FunctionalTestResults\mytestAddR11C12-ABCDE.jpg
type F
   compare with KLineEd\FunctionalTestResults\mytestAddR11C12-ABCDEF.jpg
press BS
   compare with KLineEd\FunctionalTestResults\mytestAddR11C12-ABCDE.jpg
